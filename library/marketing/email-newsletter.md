# Send the email newsletter

## Purpose

Ship the newsletter on schedule in a consistent structure, so the list stays warm without each issue being a project.

## When to use

The regular newsletter cadence [personalize: weekly, biweekly, monthly?]. NOT for one-off announcements or sales campaigns; those get their own treatment.

## Inputs

- Content candidates since the last issue: posts, wins, things learned, links worth sharing
- Email tool access [personalize: which tool: Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Resend, beehiiv?]

## Steps

1. Collect content candidates and propose the issue's main item plus 2 or 3 secondary items.
2. Draft using the standard structure (see My way).
3. Write 2 or 3 subject line options; recommend one.
4. **[APPROVAL]** Owner reviews the full issue and picks the subject line.
5. Send a test email to the owner first. Always.
6. Schedule for the standard send time. [personalize: day and time?]
7. Next day, note opens/clicks and anything unusual in one line. [personalize: where do send notes live?]
