# Write and publish a blog post

## Purpose

Get a post from idea to published and distributed in the owner's voice, with the basics (SEO, links, images) handled every time.

## When to use

Publishing to the company blog. NOT for guest posts or other people's platforms (different rules usually apply).

## Inputs

- Topic and the reader it is for
- Any source material: notes, a customer conversation, data
- CMS access [personalize: which CMS: WordPress, Ghost, Webflow, a git repo?]

## Steps

1. Outline first: working title, the one thing the reader should take away, 3 to 5 sections. **[APPROVAL]** on the outline before drafting.
2. Draft in the owner's voice (see My way). Target length: [personalize: typical word count?]
3. SEO pass: title under 60 characters, meta description, one primary phrase used naturally, 2 or 3 internal links to existing posts.
4. Add images. [personalize: image style and source: screenshots, generated, stock?]
5. **[APPROVAL]** Full draft review before publishing.
6. Publish in the CMS and verify the live page renders correctly.
7. Distribute: [personalize: where does a new post get announced: newsletter, LinkedIn, X, communities?]
