# Respond to and follow up with a lead

## Purpose

Answer inbound interest fast, qualify it honestly, and run a consistent follow-up cadence so leads stop dying of silence.

## When to use

Any inbound inquiry: form fill, email, referral intro, DM. NOT cold outbound.

## Inputs

- The inquiry itself and any context on where it came from
- Two minutes of research: their site, what they do, plausible fit

## Steps

1. Respond within the target window (see My way). Speed beats polish on first touch.
2. First reply: answer their actual question, then one or two qualifying questions (see My way), then propose a concrete next step, usually a short call with 2 or 3 specific time options.
3. Log the lead. [personalize: CRM, spreadsheet, or notes file?]
4. If no reply, run the cadence: nudge at day 3, different-angle touch at day 10, closing note at day 21, then stop.
5. **[APPROVAL]** Owner reviews replies to referrals from people who matter, and anything where fit is doubtful.

- Response-time target: [personalize: same hour? same day?]
- Qualifying questions: [personalize: the 2 or 3 things that tell you whether a lead is real, e.g. budget, timeline, who decides]
- Disqualify rules: [personalize: what makes you say no immediately?]
- The day-21 closing note leaves the door open and stops the thread. No guilt-trip language, ever.

## Edge cases

- Obvious mismatch: decline kindly in one email, refer elsewhere if someone fits, and log it as disqualified rather than ghosting.
